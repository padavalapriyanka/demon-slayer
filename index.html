The Elite Pillars: Hashira & Former Hashira

Behold the mightiest swordsmen of the Demon Slayer Corps! The Hashira are the elite of the elite, each a master of a unique breathing style, leading the fight against Muzan Kibutsuji and his demonic forces. This section also honors those who once bore the title.

Giyu Tomioka (Water Hashira)

The stoic Water Hashira, Giyu developed his own eleventh form. He is known for his calm demeanor and exceptional swordsmanship.

Shinobu Kocho (Insect Hashira)

Though lacking physical strength to decapitate demons, Shinobu is a master of poisons and a highly agile fighter, serving as the Insect Hashira.

Kyojuro Rengoku (Flame Hashira)

The enthusiastic and charismatic Flame Hashira, Kyojuro is a powerful and selfless warrior who inspires all around him with his unwavering spirit.

Tengen Uzui (Sound Hashira)

The flamboyant Sound Hashira, Tengen is a former ninja who uses his unique "Musical Score" technique in battle alongside his dual blades.

Muichiro Tokito (Mist Hashira)

A prodigious swordsman, Muichiro became a Hashira in just two months. He is quiet and often lost in thought, but terrifying in combat as the Mist Hashira.

Mitsuri Kanroji (Love Hashira)

The gentle and affectionate Love Hashira, Mitsuri possesses incredible flexibility and strength, wielding a unique, whip-like Nichirin blade.

Obanai Iguro (Serpent Hashira)

The strict and enigmatic Serpent Hashira, Obanai fights alongside his snake companion, Kaburamaru, and employs a winding, serpentine breathing style.

Sanemi Shinazugawa (Wind Hashira)

The hot-headed and aggressive Wind Hashira, Sanemi is a relentless fighter whose rare marechi blood makes him especially appealing to demons.

Gyomei Himejima (Stone Hashira)

The strongest of the current Hashira, Gyomei is a gentle giant who uses a flail and axe, and weeps for humanity's suffering. He is the Stone Hashira.

Sakonji Urokodaki (Former Water Hashira)

Tanjiro's master and a former Water Hashira, Urokodaki trained many skilled Demon Slayers and resides on Mt. Sagiri.

Jigoro Kuwajima (Former Thunder Hashira)

Zenitsu and Kaigaku's master, and a former Thunder Hashira. Despite losing a leg, he meticulously trained his successors.

Mastering the Blade: Breathing Styles & Forms

The foundation of a Demon Slayer's power lies in their Breathing Style. These unique sword techniques, derived from the Sun Breathing, allow them to mimic natural elements and amplify their physical abilities to combat demons. Explore the main styles and their devastating forms.

Water Breathing (水ノ呼吸)

Flexible and adaptable, Water Breathing utilizes fluid movements for powerful, precise slashes. Mastered by Giyu Tomioka and Tanjiro Kamado.

  • First Form: Water Surface Slash
  • Second Form: Water Wheel
  • Third Form: Flowing Dance
  • Fourth Form: Striking Tide
  • Fifth Form: Blessed Rain After the Drought
  • Sixth Form: Whirlpool
  • Seventh Form: Drop Ripple Thrust Curve
  • Eighth Form: Waterfall Basin
  • Ninth Form: Splashing Water Flow, Turbulent
  • Tenth Form: Constant Flux
  • Eleventh Form: Dead Calm (Giyu's original form)

Thunder Breathing (雷ノ呼吸)

Focuses on immense speed and rapid strikes, allowing for lightning-fast movement. Zenitsu Agatsuma is its most prominent user.

  • First Form: Thunderclap and Flash (Sixfold, Eightfold, Godspeed)
  • Second Form: Rice Spirit
  • Third Form: Thunder Swarm
  • Fourth Form: Distant Thunder
  • Fifth Form: Heat Lightning
  • Sixth Form: Rumble and Flash
  • Seventh Form: Honoikazuchi God (Zenitsu's original form)

Flame Breathing (炎ノ呼吸)

A powerful and aggressive style with strong, direct attacks and immense destructive power, typically mastered by the Rengoku family.

  • First Form: Unknowing Fire
  • Second Form: Rising Scorching Sun
  • Third Form: Blazing Universe
  • Fourth Form: Blooming Flame Undulation
  • Fifth Form: Flame Tiger
  • Ninth Form: Rengoku (Kyojuro's most powerful form)

Wind Breathing (風ノ呼吸)

Utilizes rapid, sweeping slashes that mimic strong gusts of wind, overwhelming opponents with sheer force and unpredictability. Used by Sanemi Shinazugawa.

  • First Form: Dust Whirlwind Cutter
  • Second Form: Claws-Purifying Wind
  • Third Form: Clean Storm Wind Tree
  • Fourth Form: Rising Dust Storm
  • Fifth Form: Cold Mountain Wind
  • Sixth Form: Black Wind Mountain Mist
  • Seventh Form: Gale - Sudden Gusts
  • Eighth Form: Primary Gale Slash
  • Ninth Form: Idaten Typhoon

Stone Breathing (岩ノ呼吸)

A robust and defensive style that leverages immense physical strength and specialized weapons, providing unparalleled durability. Mastered by Gyomei Himejima.

  • First Form: Serpentinite Bipolar
  • Second Form: Upper Crushing Smash
  • Third Form: Rhyolitic Deluge
  • Fourth Form: Volcanic Rock - Rapid Conquest
  • Fifth Form: Arcs of Justice

Mist Breathing (霞ノ呼吸)

Derived from Wind Breathing, it focuses on deceiving and disorienting opponents with unpredictable movements, creating openings for decisive strikes. Used by Muichiro Tokito.

  • First Form: Low Clouds, Distant Haze
  • Second Form: Eight-Layered Mist
  • Third Form: Scattering Mist Splash
  • Fourth Form: Shifting Flow Slash
  • Fifth Form: Sea of Clouds and Haze
  • Sixth Form: Lunar Dispersing Mist
  • Seventh Form: Obscuring Clouds (Muichiro's original form)

Love Breathing (恋ノ呼吸)

A unique style that utilizes a whip-like blade and incredible flexibility, combining graceful movements with powerful, far-reaching attacks. Developed by Mitsuri Kanroji.

  • First Form: Shivers of First Love
  • Second Form: Love Pangs
  • Third Form: Catlove Shower
  • Fifth Form: Wavering String of Love - Wildclaw
  • Sixth Form: Kitty Paw Love Breeze

Serpent Breathing (蛇ノ呼吸)

Characterized by twisting, coiling movements and slashes, mimicking a serpent's strike to hit opponents from unexpected angles. Used by Obanai Iguro.

  • First Form: Winding Serpent Slash
  • Second Form: Venom of the Narrow Head
  • Third Form: Coil Choke
  • Fourth Form: Twin-Headed Reptile
  • Fifth Form: Slithering Serpent Slash

Insect Breathing (蟲ノ呼吸)

A specialized style that uses a unique thrusting sword to inject various wisteria-based poisons into demons, compensating for lack of decapitating power. Developed by Shinobu Kocho.

  • Dance of the Butterflies: Caprice
  • Dance of the Hornet: Sting
  • Dance of the Dragonfly: Compound Eye Hexagon
  • Dance of the Centipede: Hundred-Legged Zigzag

Sound Breathing (音ノ呼吸)

Developed by Tengen Uzui, this flamboyant style uses explosive kunai and a "Musical Score" technique to analyze and counter enemy attacks.

  • First Form: Roar
  • Fourth Form: Constant Resounding Slashes
  • Fifth Form: String Performance

Beast Breathing (獣ノ呼吸)

Developed by Inosuke Hashibira, this wild and unpredictable style enhances his senses and allows for erratic, powerful attacks with dual blades.

  • First Fang: Pierce
  • Second Fang: Slice
  • Third Fang: Devour
  • Fourth Fang: Mince
  • Fifth Fang: Crazy Cutting
  • Sixth Fang: Palisade Bite
  • Seventh Form: Spatial Awareness
  • Eighth Fang: Explosive Rush
  • Ninth Fang: Extending Bendy Slash
  • Tenth Fang: Whirling Fangs
  • Sudden Throw: (Inosuke's original technique)

Sun Breathing (日ノ呼吸)

The original and most powerful breathing style, from which all other styles are derived. It harnesses the power of the sun to eradicate demons completely.

  • First Form: Dance
  • Second Form: Clear Blue Sky
  • Third Form: Raging Sun
  • Fourth Form: Fake Rainbow
  • Fifth Form: Fire Wheel
  • Sixth Form: Solar Heat Haze
  • Seventh Form: Beneficent Radiance
  • Eighth Form: Dragon Sun Halo Head Dance
  • Ninth Form: Setting Sun Transformation
  • Tenth Form: Burning Bones, Summer Sun
  • Eleventh Form: Solar Flare Dance
  • Twelfth Form: Flame Dance

Moon Breathing (月ノ呼吸)

A unique and deadly breathing style used by Upper Rank One, Kokushibo. It generates chaotic, crescent-shaped blades from the sword with unpredictable range and power.

  • First Form: Dark Moon - Evening Palace
  • Second Form: Pearl Flowers Moongazing
  • Third Form: Loathsome Moon - Chains
  • Fifth Form: Moon-Spirit Calamity
  • Sixth Form: Perpetual Night, Lonely Moon - Incessant
  • Seventh Form: Mirror of Misfortune - Moonlight
  • Eighth Form: Moon-Dragon Ringtail
  • Ninth Form: Descending Moon - Phases
  • Tenth Form: Drilling Slashes - Moon Through Bamboo Leaves
  • Fourteenth Form: Catastrophe - Tenman Crescent Moon
  • Sixteenth Form: Moonbow - Half Moon
