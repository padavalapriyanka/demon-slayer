## About This Project

Welcome to the **Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba Official Fan Hub**! This project is a comprehensive, fan-made website dedicated to bringing the captivating world of Demon Slayer to enthusiasts everywhere. Built with a focus on rich content, engaging visuals, and a user-friendly experience, it serves as your ultimate destination for everything related to Tanjiro, Nezuko, the Hashira, and the epic battle against demons.

The goal of this hub is to centralize information, showcase stunning visuals, and celebrate the artistry and storytelling of Koyoharu Gotouge's masterpiece.

## Features

This fan hub is packed with content and functionality:

*   **Comprehensive Character Profiles:** Detailed sections for Tanjiro, Nezuko, Zenitsu, and Inosuke, with links to even more in-depth individual pages (e.g., `character-tanjiro.html`).
*   **The Elite Hashira Showcase:** A dedicated section highlighting all the current and former Hashira, detailing their roles and key characteristics.
*   **In-depth Story Arc Overview:** Explore the pivotal sagas of Demon Slayer, from the Final Selection to the Entertainment District Arc, complete with evocative imagery.
*   **Mastering Breathing Styles:** An extensive guide to all known Breathing Styles (Water, Thunder, Flame, Wind, Stone, Mist, Love, Serpent, Insect, Sound, Beast, Sun, and even Moon Breathing), listing their forms.
*   **Interactive Hero Carousel:** A dynamic image carousel on the homepage featuring iconic characters and scenes.
*   **Media & Gallery:** A curated collection of high-resolution fan art and official visuals, with a link to a complete gallery.
*   **Latest News & Announcements:** Stay updated with a dedicated news feed for anime, manga, and merchandise.
*   **Responsive Design:** Ensures an optimal viewing experience across various devices (mobile, tablet, desktop).
*   **Semantic HTML5 & Accessibility:** Structured with ARIA attributes to enhance usability for all users.
*   **Contact Form:** A functional contact section to connect with the community.
*   **Dynamic Copyright Year:** Automatically updates the copyright year in the footer.

## Technologies Used

*   **HTML5:** For the core structure and semantic content.
*   **CSS3:** For all styling, layout, and visual presentation.
*   **JavaScript (ES6+):** For interactive elements like the hero carousel, navigation toggle, and dynamic content updates.
